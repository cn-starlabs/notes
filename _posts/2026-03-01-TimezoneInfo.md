## âš“ Global / Technical

These are the "anchors" used for technical systems and military operations.

* **UTC (Coordinated Universal Time):** The primary time standard by which the world regulates clocks and time. It does not observe Daylight Saving.
* **GMT (Greenwich Mean Time):** Practically the same as UTC, but used more in the UK and Africa.
* **Z (Zulu Time):** Used by the military and aviation; it is identical to UTC.

> **Note:** If you are scheduling a meeting, it is often safer to use **ET** or **PT** (dropping the 'S' or 'D'). This tells people "use whatever the current local time is in that region" without you having to remember if it's currently winter or summer!
